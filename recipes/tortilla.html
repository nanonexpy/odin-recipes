Tortilla Paraguaya (Paraguayan Tortilla)

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 6 oz. Paraguayan cheese
  • 2 scallions
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of flour
  • Sunflower oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl of a stand mixer, beat the eggs, then add the grated cheese and scallions.
  2. Add the flour and milk and beat until obtaining a semi-liquid preparation.
  3. Heat oil in a skillet.
  4. Pour small amounts of the mixture to form tortillas of about 4 inches (10cm) diameter.
  5. Cook on one side then turn over and cook on the other side.
